PIG DICE GAME

RULES

  • Each turn the player rolls a dice.

  • If the player gets 1 on any of the dice the player earns nothing and another player gets their turn.

  • On getting 2-6 on the dice, the player's sum is added to their turn points, until the player decides to hold their total points.

  • If the player holds their turn points, the other player gets their turn.

  • The player who gets 100 (or more) points wins the game.
